Introduction to Timber Framing - Knee Braces

Date: Saturday, November 5th from 12:00 - 3:00pm

Location: Tools For Working Wood in Sunset Park

We'll talk about mortise and tenon joinery, pegs, bent raising, and square-rule layout. From there we'll zoom in on knee braces and discuss why we need them, where they go, and how they're made.

Each student will have an opportunity to layout and cut a brace tenon. The braces we cut in this class will be the first step towards a new timber frame that will live somewhere in New York City!

Spaces are limited, reserve your spot now!

About

Brooklyn Timberworks is a team of traditional carpenters bringing the craft, fun, and community-building awesomeness of timber frame construction to New York City.

We're seeking partners who would benefit from a hand crafted timber frame shed or pavilion - we're thinking community gardens, art and maker spaces, and outdoor restaurants.

Large Timbers

Timber Framing uses large solid timbers, generally greater than 5x5 in section - this is in contrast to modern conventional framing that uses lots of standardarized dimensional lumber.

Hand Tools

Hand tools aren't strictly neccassary, but we love them! They're quite, they don't make dust, they don't break, and you don't have to plug them in. Professional timber framers use power tools to varying degrees, but in our workshops we'll be working with human power.

Traditional Joinery

Timber frame construction relies on traditional wooden joinery rather than nails and screws. A well built timber frame is a lot like a piece of fine furniture, but on a larger scale.

Assembled and Raised

All the components of a timber frame are cut to length and shape before assembly. Once that work is done the pieces can be moved to a building site and assembled in just a few hours. Frame raising is a ton of fun, can involve lots of participants, and is traditionally followed by a party under the frame.

A Finished Frame

Timber frame construction can turn a utilitarian structure like a shed into a thing of beauty.

After the Raising
